Hebrew Typing

The Society of Biblical Literature have keyboard drivers for Windows and macOS that are designed specifically for Biblical Hebrew and specifically to be easy to use for English speakers. Chose the SIL version to get the easiest layout and avoid Tiro. The Hebrew drivers that come already built into your system use a different layout which is designed for Israelis who have no prior experience of typing in English. The flashcards on Memrise also let you enter letters by clicking on the on-screen keyboard but this will probably be slower.
